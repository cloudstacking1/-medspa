FAQ's

PDO (Polydioxanone) threads are absorbable sutures used in a non-surgical procedure known as a thread lift. These threads are inserted into the skin to stimulate collagen production, leading to a natural lifting and tightening effect. The procedure is minimally invasive, with results typically visible within 3 to 4 weeks and lasting up to a year, depending on individual factors such as age and skin condition .

Chemical peels involve applying a chemical solution to the skin to exfoliate and remove damaged outer layers, promoting the growth of new healthy skin . They can address various skin concerns, including acne, sun damage, and uneven pigmentation. The recovery time and results vary based on the peel's depth: superficial peels may require minimal downtime, while deeper peels may necessitate longer recovery periods .

Facials are professional skincare treatments that cleanse, exfoliate, and nourish the skin. The process typically includes steps like cleansing, exfoliation, extractions, and mask application, tailored to individual skin types and concerns. Post-treatment, some redness or mild irritation may occur, but these effects usually subside within a few hours to a day .
